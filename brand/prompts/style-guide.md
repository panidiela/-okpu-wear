# Ọkpụ — Visual Style Guide for AI Image Generation

> This document defines the visual language to use when generating artwork for Ọkpụ using AI tools (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Ideogram, etc.).
> Internalize this before writing any prompt.

---

## The Look: Six Words

**Dark. Sculptural. Luminous. African. Contemporary. Gallery.**

If the output doesn't feel like it belongs in all six of these words, it's not right.

---

## Visual References

When generating Ọkpụ artwork, the visual DNA draws from:

### African Art Traditions
- **Benin bronzes** — intricate relief, dark bronze with gold highlights, regal portraiture
- **Igbo Ukwu metalwork** — complex geometric surface decoration
- **Kente geometry** — rhythmic, interlocking color patterns (used subtly, not literally)
- **Adinkra symbols** — West African philosophical symbols, used as texture not logo
- **Yoruba masquerade** — layered, ceremonial, powerful presence
- **Dogon sculpture** — elongated forms, abstracted human figures
- **Ndebele patterns** — bold geometric, high contrast (used sparingly)

### Contemporary Art References
- Kehinde Wiley — portraiture of Black subjects with decorative, botanical backgrounds
- El Anatsui — woven metallic assemblage, textile meets sculpture
- Wangechi Mutu — collage, organic forms, mythology and body
- Nick Cave (Soundsuits) — textile, ceremonial, performative
- Kerry James Marshall — deep black skin tones as subject, not subject of darkness

### Photographic Mood
- Seydou Keïta studio portraits — formal dignity, soft light, decorative backdrop
- Malick Sidibé — energy, authenticity, cultural pride
- Zanele Muholi — intensity, darkness, confrontational beauty

## Color Language for AI Prompts

Use these specific descriptors in prompts — they produce more consistent results than hex codes.

| Brand Color | AI Prompt Language |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Deep Black `#0B0B0B` | "deep matte black", "carbon black", "obsidian background", "near-black void" |
| Off-White `#F7F4EE` | "warm ivory", "aged parchment", "bone white", "cream" |
| Gold `#C9A45C` | "antique gold", "burnished bronze", "warm metallic gold", "aged brass highlights" |
| Terra `#A65A3A` | "terracotta", "burnt sienna", "ochre", "red earth", "laterite soil" |
| Soft Gray `#B7B2A9` | "warm ash gray", "fog", "stone gray", "pale taupe" |

## Lighting

All Ọkpụ artwork uses **dramatic, directional lighting** — not flat, not even, not ambient.

Prompt words for lighting:
- "single dramatic light source"
- "rim lighting"
- "chiaroscuro"
- "Rembrandt lighting"
- "golden hour glow from the left"
- "candlelight from below"
- "light emerging from within"

Avoid:
- "soft box lighting"
- "even ambient lighting"
- "studio white background"
- "flat illustration"

---

## Texture

Ọkpụ artwork has **physical texture**. It should feel like it could be touched.

Texture descriptors for prompts:
- "oil on canvas texture"
- "bronze casting surface"
- "aged leather"
- "linen grain"
- "ink wash on textured paper"
- "stone engraving"
- "cracked earth texture"
- "hammered metal"

---

## Figure Representation

When generating human figures:
- Skin tones: "deep brown", "rich mahogany", "dark ebony", "warm dark brown" — specific, not generic
- Expression: calm, dignified, interior — not performative, not smiling for the camera
- Proportion: slightly sculptural — influenced by African figurative art, not Western realism
- Gaze: direct and unhurried, or deliberately averted — never uncertain

---

## Composition Rules

- **Off-center subject.** Dead center feels like a product shot, not art.
- **Negative space is intentional.** Dark or textured void around the subject.
- **Vertical orientation.** Most pieces are portrait format for garment printing.
- **Edge fade.** The artwork dissolves at its boundaries — always.

---

## Style Modifiers (use freely)

These keywords consistently push results toward the Ọkpụ aesthetic:

```
fine art, gallery quality, museum quality, contemporary African art,
painterly, chiaroscuro, baroque, dramatic, ceremonial,
wearable art, limited edition, collector's piece,
textured background, organic edges, no background,
transparent edges, faded edges, dissolved border
```

---

## Anti-Patterns (never use)

These keywords actively destroy the Ọkpụ aesthetic:

```
cartoonish, cute, minimalist flat design, vector art, clip art,
cheerful, bright colors, neon, pastel, white background,
rectangular frame, logo design, T-shirt graphic, streetwear logo,
anime, manga, watercolor cute, simple icon
```
